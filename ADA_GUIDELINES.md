**Recommended:**
- Fish and seafood (especially omega-3 rich: salmon, tuna, mackerel, sardines)

- Lean cuts of beef, pork, and lamb (chuck, round, sirloin, tenderloin)
- Plant-based proteins: beans, lentils, nuts, tofu, hummus

### ‚úÖ Vegetables (ADA Best Choices)
**Non-Starchy Vegetables (Eat Freely):**
- Asparagus, broccoli, cauliflower, green beans
- Carrots, cabbage, eggplant, mushrooms
- Spinach, peppers, onions, tomatoes
- Salads with fresh vegetables

**Starchy Vegetables (Limit Portions):**
- Corn, peas, sweet potato, butternut squash
- Limit to small portions, count as carbohydrates

**How Chatita Uses This:**
- Recommends "extra vegetables" in all tips
- Suggests vegetables as fries replacement
- Highlights grilled and steamed vegetables

### ‚úÖ Fats (ADA Good Fats)
**Best Fat Sources:**
- Omega-3 fatty acids: salmon, tuna, mackerel, sardines, walnuts, flaxseeds
- Monounsaturated fats: olive oil, avocado, nuts (almonds, cashews, pecans)
- Polyunsaturated fats: canola oil, corn oil, sunflower oil

**Avoid:**
- Saturated fats: fatty meats, full-fat dairy, butter, lard
- Trans fats: fried foods, processed snacks

**How Chatita Uses This:**
- Notes "olive oil is a good fat"

- Suggests salmon (omega-3)

### ‚úÖ Grains (ADA Best Choices)
**Whole Grains (Better Choice):**
- Whole wheat bread (ask if available)
- Brown rice (if available, limit portion)
- Whole oats, quinoa, barley

- White bread, white rice, regular pasta
- If having grains, keep portions small (1/2 cup cooked)

**How Chatita Uses This:**

## ADA Fast Food Guidelines

### General Tips (Applied to All Restaurants)
1. **Order the smallest size** for sandwiches, burgers, and sides
2. **Split larger portions** like French fries
3. **Ask for salad or fruit** instead of French fries
4. **Avoid breaded and fried meats**
5. **Look up nutrition information** before you go

**How Chatita Implements:**
- "Order the smallest size available"
- "Ask for fruit or side salad instead of fries"
- "Choose grilled, baked, or steamed over fried"
- "Split fries with someone if ordering"

### Sandwiches and Burgers (ADA Guidelines)
- Order the **smallest sandwich or burger**
- Ask for **grilled instead of fried** chicken
- If grilled not available, **remove breading and skin**
- Add **extra vegetables** (lettuce, tomato, onion)
- Order **without cheese, bacon, mayonnaise** or special sauces
- Use **mustard, salsa, hot sauce, or ketchup** instead
- Get **sauces on the side** and add yourself
- Ask for **whole wheat bun** if available
- **Scoop out bread** if it's a large bun

**How Chatita Implements:**
- All American restaurant recommendations include these tips
- Burger and sandwich searches highlight these guidelines
- Tips appear for grilled chicken, steak, and fish

### Salads (ADA Guidelines)
- Ask for **grilled chicken instead of fried**
- Ask for **lowfat dressing**
- If lowfat not available, ask for **vinegar and oil mix**
- **Always ask for dressing on the side** - use less than half
- **Dip fork into dressing** then pick up food (uses less)
- Ask for salad **without cheese and bacon**
- Avoid **macaroni, potato salads** with mayo

**How Chatita Implements:**
- All salad recommendations include "dressing on the side"
- "Dip fork in dressing, then pick up food"
- "Ask for grilled chicken not fried"
- "Skip cheese and bacon or use sparingly"

### Sides (ADA Guidelines)
- Ask for **fruit or side salad** instead of fries or chips
- If ordering fries, order **small and split** with someone

**How Chatita Implements:**
- "Choose fruit or side salad instead of fries"
- "If ordering fries, get small and share"

### Drinks (ADA Guidelines)
- Drink **water or sugar-free drinks** only
- Options: unsweetened tea, coffee, light lemonade, diet soda
- **Avoid sugary drinks**: regular soda, sports drinks, sweet tea, coffee drinks with sugar

**How Chatita Implements:**
- "Drink water or sugar-free beverages only"
- Appears in every restaurant recommendation

## Cuisine-Specific ADA Applications

### Mexican Food
**ADA Guidance Applied:**
- Limit tortillas to 1-2 small corn tortillas
- Skip or limit beans to 1/4 cup
- Load up on fajita vegetables
- Use salsa instead of sour cream

### Italian Food
**ADA Guidance Applied:**
- Limit pasta to 1/2 cup if having any
- Choose marinara over cream sauces
- Focus on grilled protein and vegetables
- Ask for zucchini noodles if available

### Chinese Food
**ADA Guidance Applied:**
- Ask for steamed instead of fried
- Request sauce on the side
- Skip or limit rice to 1/3 cup
- Choose vegetables with lean protein

### Japanese Food
**ADA Guidance Applied:**
- Sashimi is excellent (lean protein, no carbs)
- Avoid tempura (fried)
- Brown rice better than white if available
- Choose grilled over fried

## Portion Control (ADA Guidelines)

### Protein Portions
- **3-4 oz cooked** = size of deck of cards
- **Fish fillet** = size of checkbook
- **Chicken breast** = palm of hand (without fingers)

**Chatita Implementation:**
- "Keep portion to 3-4 oz (size of deck of cards)"
- Notes appropriate portion sizes in tips

### Carbohydrate Portions
- **Bread**: 1 slice or scoop out extra bread
- **Rice/Pasta**: 1/3 to 1/2 cup cooked maximum
- **Beans**: 1/4 cup
- **Tortillas**: 1-2 small corn tortillas

**Chatita Implementation:**
- "Limit to 1 slice" for bread items
- "Skip rice or limit to 1/3 cup"
- "Limit tortillas to 1-2 maximum"
